💡 Suggested Additions to Your List

  • Baking powder & baking soda — essential with flour & butter
  • Yeast (active dry) — great for bread, pizza dough
  • Cornstarch — thickening sauces, soups, gravies
  • Dried lentils — faster than beans, no soaking needed
  • Bouillon / stock cubes — instant flavor base
  • Canned tomatoes (whole / crushed / paste) — endlessly useful
  • Soy sauce / Worcestershire — depth and umami
  • Dried pasta variety — multiple shapes for different dishes
  • Powdered / evaporated milk — shelf-stable dairy backup
  • Oats — breakfast, baking, thickening
  • Garlic (fresh + powder) — pantry cornerstone, both forms
  • Onion (fresh + flakes) — same principle as garlic
  • Apple cider vinegar — distinct from white vinegar, worth having both
  • Coconut milk (canned) — versatile for savory and sweet
  • Dried herbs — oregano, thyme, bay leaves, rosemary
